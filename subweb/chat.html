What factors contributed to the decline of the Tran dynasty in "Việt Nam Sử Lược" by Tran Trong Kim?

In "Việt Nam Sử Lược" by Tran Trong Kim, the decline of the Tran dynasty was contributed to by many main factors. First, economic and financial decline was caused by constant wars, especially the resistance war against Yuan-Mongol.
