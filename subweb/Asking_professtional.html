Việt Nam Sử Lược

by Tran Trong Kim

Question: What factors contributed to the decline of the Tran dynasty in "Việt Nam Sử Lược" by Tran Trong Kim?

Answer: In "Việt Nam Sử Lược" by Tran Trong Kim, the decline of the Tran dynasty was contributed to by many main factors. First, economic and financial decline was caused by constant wars, especially the resistance war against Yuan-Mongol. Second, the aristocracy and mandarins became corrupt, lived lavishly and lost the trust of the people. Third, peasant uprisings and rebel forces are increasingly common,...
